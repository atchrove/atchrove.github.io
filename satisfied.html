Satisfied

"How does it feel to be satisfied?
I have never known the feeling
Seldom do I think about my feelings
Always do I hurt others beyond compare
I hurt when I look at myself and compare
Thoughts that race a million miles per hour
My appetite for extra, a million an hour
A need for better that never gets better
Disturbed because I need to match my betters
I have come to like the taste of unrest
Inadequacy is why I feel unrest
I know little of the things that matter
I stare at the mirror and raise the matter
How does it feel to be satisfied?"
